Calligraphy & Ink

Poetry & Narrative

"Writing is the painting of the voice."
My writings fall into three categories: original fiction, literary criticism, and competition entries. This passion also deeply influences my research, inspiring my projects like Simonstown. I am currently experimenting with Human-AI Co-writing to explore new narrative boundaries.

  • Original Fiction
    Simonstown
    A dystopian romance set in a city corroded by acid rain. It explores how environmental disasters reshape human intimacy, sensory perception (smell), and social prejudice against the "Rotten People."
    Mickey V's Chocolate Factory
    A modern fable reflecting on Artificial Intelligence. Through the metaphor of a surreal chocolate factory run by an "Octopus" and an "Elephant," it questions the tension between algorithmic efficiency and artistic intuition.
  • Literary Criticism
    Monarch, General, and Literati: The Multi-Identity of Cao Pi
    Analyzing how the conflicting roles of a ruler and a poet shaped the melancholic style of Wei Dynasty poetry.
    Generation & Retrospection: A Comparative Study
    A comparative reading of Song of Youth and Goethe's Wilhelm Meister, discussing the construction of the "Bildungsroman" (Coming-of-age) genre in Chinese and German contexts.

    The Glazed Code
    A novel blending cultural heritage with sci-fi, imagining the "digital rebirth" of ancient artifacts.
