Biography

Hi! I am Bingyuan Wang, a Ph.D. candidate in the Computational Media and Arts (CMA) Thrust at HKUST(GZ), supervised by Prof. Zeyu Wang and Prof. Qifeng Chen.

With an interdisciplinary background in Electronic Engineering (B.S.) and Chinese Language and Literature (B.A.) from Peking University, I strive to understand, integrate, and reimagine traditional aesthetics and contemporary humanities through cutting-edge AI technologies.

My current research lies at the intersection of Generative AI, Affective Computing, Immersive Storytelling, and Cultural Heritage. I am passionate about unleashing the synergistic potential between Generative Models and human creativity, particularly in immersive, affective, and artistic visual content generation.
